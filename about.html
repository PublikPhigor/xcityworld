Our Story.

Xcityworld is a software development company that primarily focuses on client needs and strives to deliver result-oriented solutions. We handle every project with great diligence and provide the best solution and ROI to boost business growth. We firmly believe that we succeed when our clients succeed.

We are experts in Web Technologies, Mobile Technologies, and Digital Marketing and provide end to end solutions so that you do not have to hire multiple companies to get your project done. We have separate departments for Web Development, App Development, Digital Marketing, and On-Demand Hiring. All these departments work together to turn your project into a success story.

Located in Lagos and Ilorin Nigeria, we started as way back in 2012 with just 3 employees on board. During the spring of 2013, In the past 8 years, we have grown to more than 20 members strong team and we are climbing up on a positive growth curve.

Peter is a highly experienced Software Engineer and a calculated risk-taker, with deep Technology industry knowledge. He has a proven Executive Management track record spanning over 8 years of experience driving Company’s growth whilst spearheading high level technical collaborations in the Nigerian ICT/Digital industry.


As the CEO of Xcityworld, Peter stands at the forefront of fast moving trends in technology and is responsible for driving the vision, bringing his experience to bear on the fortunes and growth of the company while championing our core values of excellence, innovation, integrity, and boldness.


Under his leadership, Xcityworld has evolved from a small tech startup to a company that is spearheading major and strategic digital industry acquisitions to boost its nationwide footprint.
